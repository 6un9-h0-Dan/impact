There are things you can do

With great computing power comes great responsibility

Chose your provider
They don't all buy offsets, RECs or invest as much in clean energy sources. Investigate and make an informed choice.
Chose your region
Different regions are powered by differrent mixes of energy. If regulations and licenses allow you to, do select a sustainable region.
Buy Carbon Offsets
Many platforms provide easy ways to offset your emissions. You can have a great impact by pushing this within your organization. It's also worth doing it in your personal life too
Don't Grid Search
Hyperparameter search is a great source of ML-related carbon emissions. If there is no litterature to help you set good ones and you're going to look for them, at least do it randomly, don't grid search them.
Choose Clean Energy
Your organization may have a choice in their power supply. You may too. Chosing a cleaner and more sustainable source of energy will help decrease your carbon impact
Answer our poll
We're trying to understand ML practitionners' understanding and perspective on Climate Change. Answer our poll! (We know there is going to be an audience bias)
